Messy as.... Ninja Lead Project

What is Ninja Lead?

Ninja Lead is a Vietnamese slang aiming reckless motorbike drivers. There is a myth that those drivers who wear vibrant floral patterns outfits and sunglasses only choose brand Lead as their vehicles.

Brief intro about the project:

"Messy as Ninja Lead" is 3D Pointcloud interactive installation project on the "messy" aspects of life, which is the main concept of Messy as 2 (Lon Xon 2) expo.

So, what is Messy as (Lon Xon) expo?

On the online platform of Gather Town, Lon Xon Project - Cần Thơ's Independent Art Community will present art projects on the “messy” aspects of the culture of the Mekong Delta region.

The project consists of:

1. Online and Offline exhibition at Lon Xon expo.

2. A printed sticker set which is available to purchase via @lonxon.proj.

3. This website where viewers interact freely with an open source of materials such as 3D objects, animations, artworks of the project.

Lộn Xộn is the very first independent art and design exhibition project in Can Tho. Functioning as both a design studio and a community organiser, Lộn Xộn aims to build a new artistic collective for young creatives and ultimately nurture an experimental art community in Can Tho and the Mekong Delta.
